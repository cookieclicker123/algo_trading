1. **Acquisitions/M&A:** "Acquire", "Acquisition", "Merger"
   - Examples: "Reliance Global Group Signs Term Sheet to Acquire..." (+6,742%)
   - "Merck to Complete Acquisition of Cidara Therapeutics" (+103%)

2. **Phase 1 Trials** (NOT Phase 3!)
   - Phase 1 = Early stage, more speculative = bigger moves
   - Phase 3 = Later stage, priced in = smaller/loser moves

3. **Earnings/Guidance** (selective)
   - "Revenue Estimates Expected to Exceed Prior Guidance" = positive
   - "Earnings Release Date" = neutral/negative (routine)

4. **Partnerships** (when combined with surge)
   - Strategic partnerships = positive signal

2. **Phase 3 Trials**
   - 5 times in losers vs 0 in winners
   - "Phase 3 Pivotal Trial" = often priced in or disappointing

3. **Rebrand/Restructuring**
